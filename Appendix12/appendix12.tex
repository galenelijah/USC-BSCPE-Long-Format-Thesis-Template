\chapter{Original Project Deliverables and Features List}
\label{appendix:deliverables}

This appendix outlines the original deliverables and feature scope defined during the initial proposal phase of the USC Patient Information System (USC-PIS). It serves as a baseline to demonstrate the successful execution of the core requirements and the deliberate deferment of specific enterprise features to future iterations.
